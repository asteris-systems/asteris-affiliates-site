The free tier (on WordPress.org) covers small stores running a simple affiliate program. Paid tiers add unlimited affiliates, MLM, AI swipe-copy, PayPal API auto-payout, cloud-assist fraud detection, and EDD/Surecart adapters.

## Free tier

- Up to **25 active affiliates** (hard cap)
- Core commission tracking
- Manual payouts (mark commissions paid, export CSV, pay outside the plugin)
- Affiliate registration + portal
- Basic email notifications (welcome, approval, commission earned, payout sent)
- **WooCommerce only** (EDD + Surecart adapters are paid)

## What unlocks at $149/yr (Starter)

- **Unlimited affiliates** — no cap
- **MLM / two-tier referrals**
- **AI swipe-copy generator** (16 seed snippets + AI variants)
- **PayPal API auto-batch payout**
- **Cloud-assist fraud detection** (opt-in, SHA-256 hashed signals)
- **Vanity `/go/{handle}` landing pages**
- **EDD + Surecart adapters**
- **Real-time stats REST API** (cursor-paginated, transient-cached)
- **Per-product commission rates**
- **A/B email testing**
- **Bulk operations + CSV export** on all admin tables
- **Admin impersonation** ("View as" with audit log)
- **Date-range picker** on Reports + Commissions
- **Onboarding wizard** (4 steps)
- **Email throttling** (per-recipient per-template per-day cap)
- **Mobile-responsive affiliate portal**
- **WP-CLI commands**
- **Translation-ready** (.pot file)

## Pro ($299/yr) adds

- **3-site licence** (up from 1)
- **Priority email support**

## Agency ($549/yr) adds

- **10-site licence**
- **White-label affiliate portal CSS**
- **Slack support channel**
- **Migration assistance** from AffiliateWP / SliceWP / Tapfiliate

## Upgrade path

Prorated upgrade at any time. Email [support@asterisaffiliates.com](mailto:support@asterisaffiliates.com) with your licence key.

If you outgrow free (hit 25 affiliates), the plugin keeps tracking existing affiliates — new sign-ups are blocked until you upgrade.
