# Asteris Affiliates Free vs paid — what's the difference

Asteris Affiliates Free includes 6 of the 21 modules. Paid tiers (Starter, Pro, Agency, Founder) include all 20.

---

## The 6 free modules and what each version covers

**SEO module**
Free: Meta title/description editor, XML sitemap, breadcrumbs, basic schema.
Paid adds: redirect manager, AI-generated suggestions (BYO API key), multi-keyword, advanced product variation schema.

**PDF Invoices and Packing Slips**
Free: Default template, auto-generate on order completion, customer download.
Paid adds: custom branded templates, packing slips, refund credit notes, bulk export, custom merge tags.

**Variation Swatches**
Free: Colour and image swatches on variable products.
Paid adds: custom swatch shapes (rounded, pill, square), conditional swatch logic, theme-mode swatches.

**Sequential Order Numbers**
Free: Full module. Custom prefix, suffix, sequential numbering.
Paid: Same as Free. No additions.

**Product Filtering**
Free: Category and price AJAX filters on shop page.
Paid adds: faceted multi-attribute filtering, URL state preservation, mobile drawer mode, custom filter templates.

**Trust Badges**
Free: 8 pre-set badge designs on product pages and cart.
Paid adds: custom badge upload, conditional display, badge analytics.

## The 13 modules only in paid tiers

Wishlist · Quote Request · Back-in-Stock Notifications · Side Cart · Min/Max Quantity Rules · Asteris Links · Free Shipping Bar · Stock Urgency · Delivery Timeline · Feature Labels · Product Badges · AI Suite · Analytics

## Site limits by tier

Free: unlimited sites (GPL).
Starter: 1 site.
Pro: 3 sites.
Agency: unlimited sites.
Lifetime: 1 site.
