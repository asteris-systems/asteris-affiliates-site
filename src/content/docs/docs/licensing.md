# Asteris Affiliates — Licensing

Asteris Affiliates is GPL-2.0+ software. All tiers use the same open-source licence.

For the full legal text, see [/license](/license). This page covers practical questions.

---

## Site limits

| Tier | Production sites |
|------|----------------:|
| Asteris Free | Unlimited |
| Starter | 1 |
| Pro | 3 |
| Agency | Unlimited |
| Founder | 1 |

**Staging:** a staging subdomain of the same production site doesn't count as a separate site.

**Local development:** local dev environments don't count as sites.

---

## Transferring a licence

Email support@asterisaffiliates.com with your old domain and new domain. No fee. Processed within 1 business day.

Starter, Pro, Agency: transfer within your site limit.
Founder: unlimited transfers, within the same buyer's ownership.

---

## What happens when an annual licence lapses

The 13 paid-only modules deactivate. The 6 Free modules keep working. Settings are preserved — renewing restores everything.

---

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I use my licence on a client's site?**
Pro (3 sites) and Agency (unlimited) allow client site installs. Starter (1 site) is for your own site only. Agency doesn't extend to clients self-managing the plugin after delivery.

**Can I resell Asteris Affiliates licences?**
No. The licence is for your own use, not for resale or bundling.

**Is the code available to modify?**
Yes. GPL-2.0+ permits modification. If you distribute modified versions, GPL terms apply.
