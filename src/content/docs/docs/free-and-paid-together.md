# Running Asteris Free and a paid Asteris licence together

Asteris Affiliates Free and a paid tier (Starter, Pro, Agency, or Founder) run together on the same WordPress install. You don't uninstall Asteris Free to install a paid tier.

---

## How it works

Asteris Affiliates is one plugin file. The licence key activates additional modules within the same plugin.

When you purchase a paid tier:
1. Download the updated plugin from your account dashboard (it's the same plugin, with the paid module code included)
2. Upload and activate, or let the plugin update in-place if you already have the right version
3. Enter your licence key in Asteris Affiliates → Licence
4. The 13 additional paid modules appear in your module list immediately

---

## Which modules are active at each stage

| Before paid licence | After paid licence |
|---------------------|--------------------|
| 6 Free modules (toggleable) | All 21 modules (toggleable) |
| No licence required | Licence key entered and active |
| Unlimited sites | Site limit per tier |

---

## What happens if the paid licence lapses

If your annual licence isn't renewed, the 13 paid-only modules deactivate. The 6 Free modules continue working. No data is lost — paid-module settings are preserved in the database and restore when you renew.
