Asteris Affiliates is 19 WooCommerce modules in one plugin. SEO, AI-generated descriptions, PDF invoices, variation swatches, wishlist, side cart, product filtering, and 12 more. You activate the modules you need; the rest stay off.

## What's in Asteris Affiliates

21 modules. Each one toggles independently in the Asteris settings panel. Enable what your store needs; disable the rest.

**SEO module** — Custom meta titles, descriptions, breadcrumbs, schema markup, XML sitemaps, and redirects. WooCommerce-specific settings for product, category, and shop pages. If Yoast or Rank Math is already active, Asteris's SEO module steps aside automatically.

**AI Suite** — Product descriptions, meta tags, and alt text generated using your own API key. OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, and OpenRouter supported at v1.0. No monthly limits from Asteris — you own your AI bill, no markup.

**PDF Invoices and Packing Slips** — Auto-generated on order completion. Default template included in Asteris Free. Paid tiers add branded templates, refund credit notes, and bulk export.

**Variation Swatches** — Colour and image swatches on product pages in place of dropdowns. Variable products show the visual option upfront. Asteris Free includes basic swatches; paid tiers add custom shapes (pill, rounded, square) and conditional swatch logic.

**Wishlist** — Wishlist button on product and shop pages. Persists for logged-in customers. Stored in a cookie for guests. No account required to use it.

**Quote Request** — "Request a quote" button in place of or alongside "Add to cart". Sends enquiry to a configurable email address. Suitable for made-to-order or B2B stores.

**Back-in-Stock Notifications** — Customer subscribes on a sold-out product page. Asteris emails them when stock returns. No third-party email service required at v1.0 — uses WordPress's built-in mail.

**Side Cart** — Sliding cart drawer triggered by add-to-cart or cart icon click. Works on product, shop, and category pages. Stays open until dismissed.

**Sequential Order Numbers** — Custom prefix, suffix, and sequential numbering for WooCommerce orders. Replaces WooCommerce's default random order IDs. Included in Asteris Free.

**Product Filtering** — AJAX-powered filter on shop and category pages. Asteris Free includes category and price filters. Paid tiers add faceted multi-attribute filtering, URL state preservation, and mobile drawer mode.

**Min/Max Quantity Rules** — Set minimum and maximum order quantities per product or globally. Shows an inline message when a customer tries to exceed the limit.

**Asteris Links** — Link shortener and affiliate tracker built into WooCommerce admin. Create short links, track clicks. Replaces Pretty Links or ThirstyAffiliates for most stores.

**Trust Badges** — Pre-designed security and payment badges displayed on product pages and in the cart. 8 designs in Asteris Free; unlimited custom badge uploads in paid tiers.

**Free Shipping Bar** — Progress bar in the cart or header showing how much more the customer needs to spend to qualify for free shipping. Updates in real time.

**Stock Urgency** — "Only X left in stock" message on product pages. Display threshold configurable (show when stock falls below N units).

**Delivery Timeline** — Estimated delivery date displayed on product pages and in the cart. Based on configurable dispatch windows and delivery lead times.

**Feature Labels** — Custom text labels on product cards ("New", "Bestseller", "Made to order", or any custom text). Applied per product or globally.

**Product Badges** — Sale, new, and custom overlay badges on product images. Conditional display by product type, stock level, or custom rule.

**Analytics** — GA4 ecommerce event tracking for WooCommerce. Purchase, add-to-cart, remove-from-cart, and checkout events fire automatically. No Google Tag Manager required.

## Pricing

Asteris Affiliates has three paid tiers and a free version.

**Asteris Free** — 6 modules, unlimited sites. Free. On WordPress.org. No credit card.

**Asteris Starter** — 21 modules, 1 site. $149/yr launch pricing (regular $199/yr post-launch).

**Asteris Pro** — 21 modules, 3 sites. $349/yr launch pricing (regular $449/yr post-launch).

**Asteris Agency** — 21 modules, unlimited sites. $549/yr launch pricing (regular $749/yr post-launch).

**Founder pricing** = Anyone who subscribes during the launch window keeps their launch price for as long as the subscription stays active. There is no separate "Founder" SKU and no cap on Founder slots — the Founder identity is simply "you subscribed before launch closed." When launch closes (velocity-decided), regular pricing kicks in for new customers; yours doesn't change.

14-day refund on all paid tiers. No questions.
