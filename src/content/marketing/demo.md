# Asteris Affiliates — Demo

Try Asteris in your browser via WordPress Playground — no install, no signup, runs entirely in your browser. All 21 modules are enabled in a sample WooCommerce store.

[Launch the Playground demo →](#playground-embed)

First-time boot takes roughly 30–60 seconds while Playground spins up WordPress + WooCommerce + Asteris Affiliates.

---

## What's active in the demo

All 21 modules are enabled and configured for a generic retail store. The demo includes:

- Variation Swatches on variable products (colour and image options)
- Side Cart (slides open on add-to-cart)
- Product Filtering (faceted AJAX filtering on the shop page)
- Wishlist (saved within the session)
- Free Shipping Bar (progress bar in the header)
- Trust Badges (on the cart and product pages)
- Delivery Timeline (estimated delivery on each product)
- Stock Urgency (configured for low-stock display)
- Feature Labels and Product Badges (active on selected products)
- PDF Invoice generation (download after a test order)

The Playground demo loads straight into the WP admin so you can toggle modules and explore settings.

---

## What works in Playground vs what doesn't

**Works:**
- Full WordPress and WooCommerce admin
- Toggling all 21 Asteris modules on/off
- Browsing the storefront and adding sample products to cart
- Generating sample PDF invoices

**Limited or unavailable:**
- AI Suite needs your own OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, or OpenRouter API key to generate content
- No real Lemon Squeezy checkout (test mode only)
- No persistent storage — your changes reset when you close the browser tab
