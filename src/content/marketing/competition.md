## How to enter

Entry has **three required steps.** All three are necessary — there is no shortcut path.

**Step 1 — Download and install Asteris Affiliates Free** from WordPress.org on a real WooCommerce store you operate (your own or a client's, with permission). Activate the plugin.

**Step 2 — Write a 50-word answer** to: *"Why do you want Asteris Founder?"* Genuine, specific entries that reference your store and the pain points Asteris solves win. Vague, generic, or AI-generated entries are not eligible.

**Step 3 — Email your entry** to `founder@asteriscommerce.com` with the subject line `Competition entry — [your name]` and include:

- Your 50-word answer
- Your **WordPress.org username** (we verify the install via the public directory)
- The **URL of the WooCommerce store** where you installed Asteris Free

**Eligibility:** Open to anyone 18+ who runs a real WooCommerce store and has installed Asteris Free. No purchase required — Asteris Free is free on WordPress.org.

**How winners are selected:** Entries are judged on specificity, relevance, and clarity of the 50-word answer. Winners selected at the close of the Founder phase.

*This is a skill-based competition. Installing Asteris Free is required for genuine product trial — not in exchange for a review. We do not offer any incentive in exchange for posting a review of Asteris Free on WordPress.org (Guideline 8 compliant).*

No. The competition is for people who haven't purchased yet.

At the close of the Founder phase, which is velocity-dependent. No fixed date.

**How do I know if I won?**
Winners are contacted by email at the address used for the entry. We also publish a winner list on the [/founder](/founder) page after announcement.
