# Asteris Affiliates Free

**6 WooCommerce modules. On WordPress.org. No credit card.**

Asteris Affiliates Free is the free version of Asteris Affiliates. It includes 6 of the 21 modules and installs directly from the WordPress plugin directory.

[Install Asteris Affiliates Free from WordPress.org →](https://wordpress.org/plugins/asteris-affiliates)

---

## What's in Asteris Affiliates Free

### SEO module (basic)

Custom meta titles and descriptions for products, categories, shop, and archive pages. XML sitemap. Breadcrumb support for WooCommerce product hierarchies.

The paid-tier SEO module adds: AI-generated title and meta suggestions (uses your API key), multi-keyword optimisation, redirect manager, advanced schema for product variations and reviews.

### PDF Invoices and Packing Slips (basic)

PDF invoice auto-generated on order completion. One default template. Customers download from their account order page. Admin downloads from the order screen.

The paid-tier PDF module adds: custom branded templates, packing slips, refund credit notes, bulk export, custom field merge tags.

### Variation Swatches (basic)

Colour and image swatches on variable product pages in place of dropdown menus. Configured per attribute in the WooCommerce attribute settings.

The paid-tier Variation Swatches module adds: custom swatch shapes (rounded, pill, square), per-attribute swatch sets, conditional swatch logic, theme-mode (light/dark) swatch styling.

### Sequential Order Numbers

Custom prefix, suffix, and sequential numbering for WooCommerce orders. Replaces WooCommerce's default random order ID format with clean sequential numbers.

Full module — no paid-tier additions. This module is identical in Asteris Free and paid tiers.

### Product Filtering (basic)

AJAX-powered shop and category page filtering by category and price. No page reload. Works with standard WooCommerce product loops.

The paid-tier Product Filtering module adds: faceted multi-attribute filtering (filter by colour, size, custom attributes), URL state preservation (shareable filtered URLs), mobile drawer mode, custom filter widget templates.

### Trust Badges

8 pre-designed security and payment trust badges for product pages and the cart. Display position configurable in settings.

The paid-tier Trust Badges module adds: custom badge image upload, conditional display (per product, per user role), badge analytics (impressions and clicks).

## Running Asteris Free and paid together

Asteris Affiliates Free and a paid Asteris licence can run on the same site simultaneously. Asteris Free handles the 6 free modules; the paid licence unlocks the other 14.

You don't need to uninstall Asteris Free to install a paid tier. The licence key activates within the same plugin.

## Requirements

WordPress 6.4 or later. WooCommerce 8.0 or later. PHP 8.1 or later. Minimum 128MB PHP memory (256MB recommended for stores with 5,000+ products).

Tested on Storefront, Astra, Kadence, GeneratePress, Blocksy, and Hello Elementor themes.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Asteris Affiliates Free really free, permanently?**
Yes. It's on WordPress.org under a GPL licence. Asteris Commerce doesn't charge for the free tier, now or in the future.

**Do I need to create an account to use Asteris Free?**
No. Install directly from the WordPress plugin repository. No account on this site required.

**Will Asteris ever remove modules from the free tier?**
No. The 6 free modules are locked. Asteris may add modules to Asteris Free in the future but won't remove what's already there.

**Can I use Asteris Affiliates Free on a client site?**
Yes. GPL-licensed. Use it on client sites without restriction. If the client needs paid modules, they (or you) purchase a paid tier.

**Does Asteris Free receive security updates?**
Yes. Security updates apply across all tiers including Free.

**How do I get support for Asteris Free?**
WordPress.org support forum is the primary channel for free-tier users. Email support@ is a fallback but response times are 2–3 business days via that route.
