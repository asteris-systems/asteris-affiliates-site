## Why "locked for life" instead of one-time-lifetime

Earlier drafts of this page sold a $249 one-time Founder Lifetime licence. We retired that model on 2026-06-03 for three reasons that matter to anyone considering subscribing:

1. **One-time lifetime deals fail customers when the vendor fails.** Plugin vendors that took one-time payments in 2018-2020 (PromiseGenius, Polymail, several WP coupon plugins) collapsed by 2024 because they had infinite support obligation against zero recurring revenue from their lifetime tier. We don't want to be that story. Recurring revenue from Founders — even discounted — funds the support those Founders actually need.
2. **Compounding savings is the better deal anyway.** A Founder Agency customer locked at $549/yr while regular Agency rises to $749 and eventually $899 is saving $200, then $350 per year — compounding to $1,500+ over five years without doing anything. A one-time $249 payment doesn't compound. The longer you stay, the better Founder pricing looks.
3. **Two-track pricing beats three-track confusion.** Killing the separate "Founder Lifetime" SKU removes the "which tier am I supposed to pick" question entirely. Pick Starter / Pro / Agency by seat count. Subscribe now to lock launch pricing. That's the whole decision.

## FAQ

### What happens to my price if I upgrade from Starter to Pro mid-subscription?

You move to the locked Pro price ($349/yr) — that's a $200/yr step up from locked Starter ($149/yr). The launch lock follows you across tiers as long as you stay subscribed.

### What happens to my price if I cancel and re-subscribe later?

You re-subscribe at the current regular price ($199 / $449 / $749 once launch is closed). The launch-lock is a *continuous-subscription* benefit, not a *one-time grandfather* benefit. So don't cancel.

### Does the launch lock apply to monthly subscriptions too?

Yes. Founder Starter monthly is $17/mo locked; Pro $39/mo locked; Agency $59/mo locked. The same continuous-subscription rule applies — cancel and re-subscribe later → current regular monthly price.

### How long is the launch window open?

No fixed date — duration is velocity-decided. The launch closes when Nick judges the public-launch news cycle has crested. We deliberately don't publish a countdown date because we'd rather hit a deadline than miss one. When it closes, it closes; new customers pay regular pricing.

### Will regular pricing be the only price after launch closes, or will there be other discounts?

After launch closes, regular pricing ($199 / $449 / $749) becomes the only price for new customers. There may be occasional promotional pricing on Black Friday or similar events — but those are short windows, not new "Founder" tiers. We won't reopen this launch-lock window.
