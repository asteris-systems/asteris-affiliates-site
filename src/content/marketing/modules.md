# Asteris Affiliates — All 21 modules

Asteris Affiliates is one plugin with 20 independently-toggleable modules. Enable what your store needs; leave the rest off. All modules share one settings panel, one update cycle, and one licence.

Modules marked ✦ are included in Asteris Affiliates Free. All 21 modules are included in Starter, Pro, Agency, and Founder.

[See tier pricing →](/pricing) · [Compare Free vs paid →](/docs/free-vs-paid)

---

## SEO module ✦ (basic in Free)

**What it does:** Custom meta titles, descriptions, breadcrumbs, XML sitemaps, and Open Graph tags for WooCommerce products, categories, shop page, and archives. Schema markup for products (price, availability, reviews).

**What paid adds over Free:** AI-generated title and meta suggestions using your own API key, multi-keyword optimisation, redirect manager (301, 302, 410), advanced schema for product variations and bundled reviews.

**Replaces:** Yoast SEO Premium ($129/yr), Rank Math PRO ($59/yr), Yoast WooCommerce SEO add-on ($69/yr)

**Compatibility note:** If Yoast SEO or Rank Math is already active on the site, Asteris Affiliates's SEO module steps aside. No conflict; the two don't run simultaneously.

[SEO module documentation →](/docs/modules/seo)

## AI Suite

**What it does:** Generates product descriptions, meta titles, meta descriptions, and image alt text using your own OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, or OpenRouter API key. Bulk-generate across multiple products from the WooCommerce product list screen.

**Limits:** None from Asteris Affiliates. Your AI provider bills you directly based on your usage. No markup from Asteris.

**API providers supported at v1.0:** OpenAI (GPT-4o, GPT-4o mini), Anthropic (Claude Sonnet), Google Gemini (Gemini 1.5 Pro, Gemini 1.5 Flash), and OpenRouter (access to 100+ models through a single key). OpenAI-compatible local endpoints (Ollama, LM Studio) planned for v1.1.

**Replaces:** No direct single-plugin equivalent. Comparable AI content tools: Journalist AI (varies), Writesonic for WooCommerce (varies).

[AI Suite documentation →](/docs/modules/ai-suite)

## PDF Invoices and Packing Slips ✦ (basic in Free)

**What it does:** Auto-generates a PDF invoice on order completion. Customers download from their My Account order page. Admin downloads from the WooCommerce order screen.

**What paid adds over Free:** Custom branded templates (upload your logo, set colours), packing slips (separate from invoice), refund credit notes, bulk PDF export from the orders screen, custom merge-tag fields (PO number, custom notes).

**Replaces:** WP Overnight PDF Invoices Premium ($79/yr, now under UpdraftPlus)

[PDF Invoices documentation →](/docs/modules/pdf-invoices)

## Variation Swatches ✦ (basic in Free)

**What it does:** Replaces WooCommerce dropdown menus on variable products with colour swatches, image swatches, or label swatches. Configured per-attribute in WooCommerce product settings.

**What paid adds over Free:** Custom swatch shapes (rounded, pill, square), per-attribute swatch sets, conditional swatch logic (show/hide swatches based on other attribute selections), theme-mode (light/dark) swatch styling.

**Replaces:** YITH Color, Image & Label Variation Swatches ($86/yr), WooCommerce Variation Swatches ($99/yr)

## Asteris Coupons

**What it does:** Extends WC's built-in coupons (never replaces them) with the four advanced rule types that normally require a stack of separate plugins:

- **BOGO** — buy X get Y free or discounted, mix-and-match across products, with a server-side quantity lock that mitigates the WebToffee-class exploit pattern from 2025 (where customers could manipulate cart quantity to claim more free items than the rule allowed).
- **Tiered** — spend $50 get 5%, spend $100 get 10%, etc. Highest matching tier wins. Either % off or fixed $ off per tier.
- **URL coupons** — `?coupon=CODE` auto-applies via a 30-day cookie that survives cart abandonment. IP rate-limited to prevent code brute-forcing.
- **Scheduled + segmented** — date / day-of-week / time-of-day windows; role / category / first-time-buyer / repeat-buyer-min-orders gates.

Single-coupon-per-cart with deterministic top-down evaluation (follows the WC Dynamic Pricing precedent). Includes a 4-tab metabox on every WC coupon edit screen, a plain-English rule summary on the coupons list table, a one-click migration scanner that imports existing WC coupons into Asteris management, and an inline preview simulator that runs the real evaluator against a synthetic cart so you can verify rules before publishing.

Shares its cart-spend thresholds with the Free Shipping Bar module via a published filter (`asteris_cart_thresholds`) — so a customer who's $5 short of "free shipping at $50" and $15 short of "10% off at $60" sees both in the bar, from one source of truth.

**Replaces:** Advanced Coupons Pro ($99.50/yr first year, $199/yr renewal) + WebToffee Smart Coupons ($89/yr) + Flycart Discount Rules Pro ($85/yr) — typically stacked at $180–$400+/yr.

[Coupons documentation →](/docs/modules/coupons)

## Live Monitor

**What it does:** Real-time visitor dashboard with WooCommerce cart awareness. See who's on the site right now, what page they're on, what's in their cart, and which carts are about to abandon — before they leave.

- **Live cart visibility** — every visitor's cart contents (products, quantities, line totals, coupons) updated every 10 seconds via a first-party JS heartbeat
- **Customer identity** — logged-in WC customer name, email, prior order count, lifetime value, last order date (1-hour cached)
- **Funnel stage tracking** — browsing / cart / checkout / payment / complete, auto-detected from URL + cart state
- **One-shot operator coupon push** — operator clicks visitor → picks template → server mints a single-use, IP-bound, TTL'd WooCommerce coupon → next heartbeat surfaces it as a floating toast in the shopper's browser within 10 seconds. Customisable colours, position, template content, percent. The coupon URL leaked to social media can't be redeemed by anyone except the original recipient — defence-in-depth.
- **Cart-stall auto-trigger** — define a rule ("cart ≥ $200 AND idle > 60s AND at /checkout") and Asteris auto-fires a pre-approved template + minted coupon. No operator needed. The "what if I'm asleep?" feature. Per-session cooldown so the same cart doesn't get nudged every cron tick.
- **Pre-email abandon detection** — flags carts as abandoning BEFORE checkout email is entered. Score = idle × cart_value × stage_weight. Most recovery tools wait until they have an email — Asteris doesn't.
- **Alert engine** — 9 rules covering high-value cart idle, abandoning checkout, VIP-on-site, flash-sale spike, plus carding/fraud detection (declined-transaction spikes, AVS/CVV failure clusters, multi-card-from-one-IP, off-hours micro-baskets, identical-UA-many-IPs). 5 channels: in-admin · email · Slack Block Kit webhook · generic JSON webhook (HMAC-SHA256 signed) · **auto-nudge** (fires a templated nudge to the matched session).
- **Real-shopper viewer counter** — `[asteris_live_viewers]` + `[asteris_live_added_to_cart]` shortcodes. Real heartbeat-derived numbers, configurable floor, no randomisation. "Real numbers. Never randomised. Never faked." — competitors literally have a randomise toggle in their app-store listings; Asteris can't because the numbers come from the actual heartbeat.
- **Ad-blocker visibility delta** — hero tile shows "Asteris saw 47 sessions today. GA4 saw 32. You're blind to 32% of your real traffic." Operator can sync their real GA4 number for exact delta, or use the industry-average estimate. The single most virally-shareable stat in the product.
- **Site Health integration** — 3 tests in Tools → Site Health: alert-engine cron firing, Asteris real-time vs WC Analytics action-scheduler queue depth, HPOS + Blocks Checkout compatibility.
- **Self-hosted + GDPR-clean** — no SaaS pipeline, no 3rd-party data sharing. IP hashed at 24h, never raw. GDPR consent respected. Ad-blocker-immune (first-party heartbeat survives where GA4 fails).
- **Activity Log audit** — every admin view of identifying info, every nudge sent, every alert fired is logged. GDPR Article 30 records-of-processing ready.

**Replaces:** Metorik (USD $300+/yr SaaS) + WP Statistics Realtime Stats (USD $39+/yr) + MonsterInsights Plus (USD $100/yr) + carding-fraud detection (typically Sift / Signifyd at $500–$2000/mo enterprise) + on-PDP social-proof widget (Provely / Nudgify $9–$65/mo) — typically **$300–$2000+/yr** for what Asteris ships as part of the plugin.

## What Asteris Affiliates doesn't include

21 modules covers a broad slice of a WooCommerce store's needs. It doesn't cover dropshipping, subscriptions, memberships, payment gateways, tax automation, or sales funnels. Those categories have dedicated plugins that work alongside Asteris Affiliates.
