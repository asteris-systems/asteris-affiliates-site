# Asteris Affiliates — Pricing

Asteris Affiliates has one free tier and three paid tiers. **All paid tiers include all 21 modules** — the only difference is how many sites you can install on.

**Launch pricing is open.** Anyone who subscribes during the launch window keeps the launch price locked for as long as their subscription stays active. When the launch closes (velocity-decided, no fixed date), new customers pay regular pricing. Your locked price doesn't change.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there a monthly billing option?**
Yes. Starter $17/mo, Pro $39/mo, Agency $59/mo. Monthly subscribers also lock the launch monthly rate for as long as the subscription stays active. There is no separate "lifetime" one-time option — Founder pricing means *launch price locked for as long as you stay subscribed.*

**What happens when my annual licence expires?**
Asteris Affiliates continues to work on your site but stops receiving updates and support. Renew to resume updates and support access. If you renew within the same subscription, your locked launch price continues.

**What happens to my price if I cancel and re-subscribe later?**
You re-subscribe at the current regular price ($199 / $449 / $749 once launch is closed). The launch-lock is a *continuous-subscription* benefit, not a *one-time grandfather* benefit. So don't cancel.

**Can I switch between tiers?**
Yes. Upgrades are pro-rated. Downgrades take effect at the next renewal date. Email support@ to initiate. Your launch-lock follows you across tiers as long as you stay subscribed.

**Can I transfer my licence to a different domain?**
Yes. Starter, Pro, and Agency licences transfer within the site count for your tier. Email support@ — no fees, no friction.

**What does the 14-day refund cover?**
Any paid tier, purchased directly from asterisaffiliates.com, within 14 days of purchase. Processed within 24 hours of your request. See [Refund Policy](/refund-policy) for full terms; email support@ with your order ID to start a refund.

**Does Asteris Free require a licence key?**
No. Asteris Affiliates Free installs from WordPress.org like any other plugin. No account required.

**Is the Agency tier white-label?**
No. Asteris Affiliates branding remains in the WP admin. Agency is for unlimited site installs, not for reselling or white-labelling.

**What licence type is Asteris Affiliates?**
GPL-2.0+. The plugin code is GPL, consistent with WordPress's own licence requirements.

**What payment methods are accepted?**
Card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and Apple Pay / Google Pay where supported — via Lemon Squeezy.

**Are prices in USD?**
Yes. All prices are USD. Lemon Squeezy handles currency display and tax collection (including Australian GST for AU customers and VAT for EU customers).

**Why is there no separate Lifetime tier?**
The earlier $249 one-time Lifetime tier was retired on 2026-06-03. Reasons explained in detail on the [Founder pricing page](/lifetime). Short version: recurring subscription at a locked launch price funds the support those customers actually need, and the compounding savings (vs regular pricing rising over time) is a better deal than a one-time payment.
